About

The 90s was a decade defined by many different fashions, music, genres and styles. From the underground music scenes of punk, grunge and rave to pop culture moments such as Clueless and Tamagotchis, the 90s were a melting pot of different trends. With the creation of Photoshop 1.0 for Macintosh in 1990, the evolution of 90s design had a huge impact on not just the decade in question, but also the graphic design trends from Anti Design, 90s Grunge, and Rave and all the way to Pop Culture.

I want Sherin Snidal Design to show that 90s theme of contradicting design styles. The 90s was all about experimenting with design and throwing the classic rules out the window and that is exactly how I feel about what the purpose of design is about; never stick with the boring.
